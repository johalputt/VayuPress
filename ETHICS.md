# VayuPress Ethics

## Ethical Principles

VayuPress is committed to these non-negotiable ethical principles:

1. **Privacy by Design** — User data is private by default. No telemetry, no tracking, no data harvesting.
2. **Transparency** — All decisions impacting users are transparent and documented.
3. **Accessibility** — VayuPress must be accessible to all users regardless of ability (WCAG 2.2 AA).
4. **No Surveillance** — VayuPress will never include features that enable surveillance or user tracking.
5. **Ethical AI** — AI features are optional, transparent, and respect user autonomy.
6. **No Dark Patterns** — No deceptive UI/UX elements that manipulate users.
7. **Sustainability** — Efficient resource usage; minimal environmental footprint.
8. **Inclusivity** — The VayuPress community is inclusive and welcoming to all.

## VayuPress Ethical AI Charter

All AI features must comply with:

1. **User Consent** — AI features are opt-in and clearly labeled.
2. **Transparency** — Users are informed when AI generates or modifies content.
3. **No Training on User Data** — VayuPress never trains models on user content.
4. **Local-First** — Local models (Ollama, Llama.cpp) preferred over cloud APIs.
5. **Bias Mitigation** — AI features are designed to minimize bias and discrimination.
6. **Accountability** — AI decisions are explainable and auditable.
7. **No Autonomous Actions** — AI never takes actions without explicit user approval.

## Ethical Review Process

Major changes must undergo ethical review if they:
- Involve user data
- Use AI or machine learning
- Impact accessibility
- Could enable surveillance or tracking
- Have significant environmental impact

**Review Board**: A rotating panel of 3 maintainers (including the Ethics Lead).

**Process**:
1. Submit an ethical impact assessment with the RFC or PR.
2. Review Board evaluates and approves or rejects based on alignment with principles above.

## Ethical Violation Reporting

Email: **ethics@vayupress.com** (PGP optional)

- Acknowledged within 24 hours.
- Critical violations trigger immediate action (e.g., removal of offending feature).
- Violations and resolutions are disclosed publicly (with redactions for privacy).

## Annual Ethical Metrics

Published annually:
- Privacy compliance (GDPR, CCPA)
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.2 AA)
- AI usage transparency
- Environmental impact
- Community inclusivity
