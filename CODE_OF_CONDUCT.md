# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

VayuPress is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and considerate in language and actions
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the community, not just individuals
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Unethical AI usage (generating harmful content, surveillance tools)
- Any conduct that violates VayuPress's [Ethical Principles](ETHICS.md)

## Enforcement

**Violations are handled in three steps:**

| Step | Trigger | Consequence |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Warning | First violation | Private written warning, explanation |
| Temporary Ban | Repeated or serious violation | 7-day ban from all community spaces |
| Permanent Ban | Severe, sustained, or unethical violations | Permanent removal |

## Reporting

Report violations to **community@vayupress.com**.

All reports are reviewed within 48 hours. Reporters' identities are kept confidential. Retaliating against reporters is itself a violation.

For ethical violations (surveillance, harassment, discrimination, unethical AI usage): **immediate permanent ban** — no prior warning required.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all VayuPress community spaces: GitHub (issues, PRs, discussions), email lists, Discord/Slack (if applicable), and any event where someone represents VayuPress.
